Most users notice softer, smoother lips within 3–5 days of consistent use. For severely chapped lips, applying at night and again in the morning typically shows visible repair within one week. For the best results, use it as part of both your morning and bedtime routine.

Yes — PETA certified cruelty-free. No animal testing is conducted at any stage of production. The formula is primarily plant-based; if you follow a strict vegan lifestyle, please review the full ingredient list, as some Himalaya variants may contain beeswax.

Petroleum jelly (petrolatum) sits on the surface — it doesn't nourish or absorb into your skin. Himalaya's botanical oils penetrate the lip barrier, delivering vitamins and fatty acids that actively repair and build moisture from within. The natural oils — particularly carrot seed oil — provide a low level of natural UV protection that's adequate for everyday indoor and casual outdoor use. For prolonged direct sun exposure, we recommend pairing this with a dedicated SPF lip balm for full protection.
